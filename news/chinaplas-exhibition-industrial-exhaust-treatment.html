Exhibition Update

KangYu EnTech Showcases Industrial Exhaust Treatment Solutions at CHINAPLAS

KangYu EnTech presented industrial exhaust treatment systems and process route solutions for rubber, plastic and recycling industries at CHINAPLAS. The booth highlighted process-route thinking for rubber and plastic industry exhaust, including oil fume purification, smoke treatment and staged gas cleaning.

CHINAPLAS Rubber & Plastic Industry Industrial Exhaust Treatment

Process route presentation

KangYu EnTech presented integrated treatment logic for rubber and plastic exhaust, including pre-treatment, oil fume purification and downstream VOC / odor control routes.

Equipment display

The booth displayed a complete industrial exhaust treatment system model with ducting, filtration modules and platform structure for customer communication.

Customer communication

The KangYu team communicated with visitors and partners about plastic recycling fumes, rubber processing exhaust, oil mist, smoke and odor control requirements.
